Strictly Limited Return & Refund Policy

STRICT RETURN POLICY NOTICE:
We operate under a strict Damaged-On-Arrival Return Policy. We ONLY accept returns, exchanges, or refunds if the product arrives damaged due to shipping or transit. Returns based on buyer's remorse, change of mind, incorrect ordering, or normal wear and tear are strictly not accepted.

4.1 Eligibility Criteria for Returns

To qualify for a return, replacement, or refund, all of the following conditions must be met:

  • Physical Damage in Transit: The item must have sustained visible physical damage during shipping.
  • Reporting Window: Damaged items must be reported to our customer support team within 48 hours of delivery. Claims made after 48 hours will not be honored.
  • Unused Condition: The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and with all tags, accessories, and manuals intact.
  • Proof of Damage Required: Clear photo and/or video evidence of the damaged package and item must be provided upon submitting a claim.

4.2 Non-Returnable Items & Exclusions

We do not accept returns under any of the following circumstances:

  • Change of mind or accidental purchases.
  • Damage resulting from misuse, improper installation, or handling after delivery.
  • Minor cosmetic box damage that does not affect the functionality of the product inside.
  • Requests submitted after the mandatory 48-hour post-delivery window.

How to Submit a Return Claim

If your package arrives damaged, please follow this step-by-step procedure to ensure prompt processing of your request:

  1. Inspect Your Order Immediately: Upon receiving your package, inspect the outer carton and product inside before discarding packaging materials.
  2. Document the Damage: Take clear, high-resolution photographs and a short video showing the outer shipping label, shipping carton, and damaged product.
  3. Contact Support: Email support@example.com within 48 hours with your Order ID, tracking number, photos/videos, and a brief explanation of the damage.
  4. Claim Review: Our Quality Assurance team will review your submission within 1–2 business days.
